1 Config Maps {#configmaps}
2 ===========
3 
4 ## Overview
5 
6 The ConfigMap is a nested implementation of std::maps and std::vectors to provide a python dictionary-like data storage structure. Datatypes that can be stored are:
7 
8  - std::string
9  - int
10  - unsigned int
11  - unsinged long
12  - double
13  - bool
14 
15 An important feature is the loading and storing of ConfigMaps in yaml files. The example shows the usage of ConfigMaps for further details check [ConfigMap](@ref mars::utils::ConfigData.h).
16 
17 Note: While reading and writing yaml files the order of dictionaries is not necessarily kept.
18
